Operating model

Governed first. Autonomous only after proof.

Continuum begins in supervised mode. It recommends, explains, drafts, and escalates. Execution authority is earned through benchmarked alignment and explicit control boundaries.

Doctrine

Decision systems need continuity without losing accountability.

The principle

A digital instance should not simply answer. It should operate inside a defined scope, with clear constraints, audit trails, and human override.

The standard

Every decision must expose why it was made: which rule applied, what memory was used, what risk was detected, and whether execution is permitted.
